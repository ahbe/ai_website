About The Academy

“I visualize a time when we will be to robots what dogs are to humans, and I’m rooting for the machines.”

Claude Shannon


As part of our annual program and our mission as a proactive organization that always predicts the future and tries to stay ahead of technological advancement, we IEEE ENICarthage Student Branch are aiming to organize an AI Academy that will help students to acquire theoretical knowledge and practical skills in order to start working on AI projects.


This program will be finished by an AI Hackathon where not only participants of the academy but anyone interested in the topic will compete using AI Technologies to solve a certain problem that will be announced the day of the hackathon.
